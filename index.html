Vist — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Welcome to Vist ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application Vist: Reusable & Sharable Checklists ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, and password.
  • Profile information: You may optionally provide your full name, bio, avatar photo, and country.
  • Content: Checklist templates, steps, notes, comments, and other content you create within the App.

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage data: How you interact with the App, including lists you create, execute, like, save, and comment on.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, and app version.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide and improve the App's features
  • Display your public lists and profile to other users
  • Enable social features such as likes, saves, and comments
  • Send you important account-related notifications
  • Ensure the security and integrity of the App

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Your information may be shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • Public content: Lists and profiles you mark as public are visible to all users of the App.
  • Service providers: We use Supabase (supabase.com) as our backend infrastructure provider for database storage and authentication. They process data on our behalf under their own privacy policy.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Camera and Photo Access

The App requests access to your camera and photo library solely to allow you to upload a profile avatar photo. We do not access your camera or photos without your explicit permission. Photos are stored securely on our servers and are only used to display your profile picture within the App.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure with industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address below. Upon request, we will delete your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Portability: Request a portable copy of your data.
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

9. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — Database, authentication, and file storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Expo — App development platform (Privacy Policy)

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
